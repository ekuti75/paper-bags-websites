packaging paper bag

Along with plastics, paper is currently one of the most widely used type of packaging. The advantage of paper boxes is good storage, strength and stackability and relatively low weight. Corrugated cardboard packaging is particularly good protection for goods against mechanical damage, so it is often used as group packaging for the transport of several units of packaged items. Paper packaging isn’t suitable for storing liquids, it doesn’t prevent the penetration of gases. Cardboard boxes are a suitable barrier against UV radiation.
